Bernard and Denise Coley's Journey

"My advice to care partners is to educate yourself about PD and to find the silver linings in tasks you will have to undertake to support your Parkinson’s person. I am in the best physical shape that I have been in many years because I engage in regular fitness activities with Denise.

I turned the daily task of preparing the family meals into an international food tour, learning to make nutritious comfort food meals from recipes around the world. Artistic plating and serving presentations help Denise deal with a loss of appetite and increase her interest in eating nutritional foods."
